BridgePoint Engineering Rules

1. Never replace working architecture.

2. Extend existing modules.

3. Keep components provider independent.

4. Every feature must be reusable.

5. Every feature must be documented.

6. Prefer composition over duplication.

7. Keep UI consistent with the BridgePoint Design System.

8. Do not introduce unnecessary dependencies.

9. Minimize technical debt.

10. Every change must improve long-term maintainability.

11. Before every implementation:
    - Analyze
    - Explain
    - Estimate impact
    - Wait for approval

12. After every implementation:
    - Review your own work
    - Identify possible regressions
    - Suggest future improvements
